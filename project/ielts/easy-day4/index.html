雅思语料库 · 简单版 - Day 4

简单版 — C20 Test 4 (Personal Qualities / News / Discussion)

一、Part 1: Personal Qualities

Q1: What are your best personal qualities?

“I’d say self-reflection. I believe that looking at yourself honestly and asking what you could do better is the best way to level up as a person. I do this regularly.”

Q2: Do you have the same qualities as your parents?

“Yes, we’re all pretty meticulous. My parents always said you need to pay attention to details. They didn’t just say it — they actually practiced what they preached. That habit just rubbed off on me.”

Q3: What qualities are important in a friend?

“Sincerity and kindness. Everyone has flaws, and friends will disagree sometimes. But as long as their core nature is good and honest, I can look past the small things.”

Q4: Do you have the qualities to be a good leader?

“Honestly, not really — not yet, anyway. I’m still missing some key qualities. But I aspire to get there, so I’m always trying to learn and improve.”

二、Part 2: Describe a long discussion about a news story

“I’d like to talk about when IG won the League of Legends World Championship. I was in high school, and everyone talked about it for days.

The moment they won, every guy in class came in asking, ‘Did you watch? IG is insane!’ It was huge because it was the first time a Chinese team had won. We’d been following for years with so many disappointments. It finally felt like the world was hearing our story.

Even our teacher brought it up in class. We just couldn’t stop talking about this historic moment we’d all been waiting for.”

三、Part 3: News & Discussion programmes

Q1: How do people get news in your country?

“Mostly through mobile phones. Digital platforms have become the main way people consume news, from the elderly to younger people. Smartphones have basically replaced traditional media.”

Q2: Are people more interested in local or national news?

“Both. Local news is about what’s happening around you. But national news matters too — things like economic policy or education directly affect your future.”

Q3: How important is international news?

“For most people, it’s not essential. But if you work in an international field, or want to work globally, keeping up with international news is pretty important.”

Q4: Why are discussion programmes with ordinary people popular?

“Because they feel real. When someone talks about their actual life, the audience thinks, ‘That’s exactly how I feel.’ It creates an instant connection. People feel like their voice matters.”

Q5: What kinds of people join discussion programmes?

“Some people just love sharing their views. Others have been through something and want to get their story heard. And some simply enjoy being on camera.”

Q6: Do discussion programmes influence people positively or negatively?

“It depends. Good programmes can broaden perspectives and help people understand different experiences. But some programmes just create drama and may actually divide people more.”

四、核心词汇

  • self-reflection — 自省
  • level up — 提升进步
  • meticulous — 一丝不苟的
  • rubbed off on me — 耳濡目染
  • look past — 不计较
  • aspire to — 渴望
  • historic moment — 历史时刻
  • supplanted — 取代了
  • economic policy — 经济政策
  • instant connection — 即时共鸣
  • get their story heard — 让故事被听到
  • broaden perspectives — 拓宽视野
