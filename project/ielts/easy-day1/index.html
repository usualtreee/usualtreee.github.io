雅思语料库 · 简单版 - Day 1

简单版 — C20 Test 1 (Walking / Film / Theatres)

一、Part 1: Walking

Q1: How much walking do you do in your daily life?

“Not much. On weekdays I ride an e-scooter around campus. But on weekends I like doing city walks in Shanghai. It’s such a comfortable city to explore on foot.”

Q2: Did you walk more when you were at school than now?

“Yeah, definitely. In my undergrad the campus was huge and I lived on the sixth floor with no elevator. So I had to climb stairs every day. Now I ride an e-scooter everywhere, so my walking has really dropped.”

Q3: What places are there to go for a walk near where you live?

“I usually take the subway to the Bund or Lujiazui. I love the mix of old and new buildings there. It’s always bustling with tourists and feels really lively.”

Q4: Would you ever like to go on a walking holiday?

“I’d love to. My schedule is pretty busy right now, but I think you should do things you want while you’re young. So I’m thinking of squeezing in a short trip on a weekend rather than putting it off.”

二、Part 2: Describe a film you’d like to see again

“The film I’d love to see again is Avengers: Endgame. I first watched it in high school with my classmates. It was a big deal for all of us.

I’m not usually into superhero movies, but this one opened the door to Marvel films for me. My friends were absolutely obsessed with the characters, and they said the final battle was insanely satisfying.

But for me, the reason I want to see it again is more about the feeling I had back then. I was young and carefree. Sitting in that cinema with close friends made me feel like anything was possible.

If I had the chance, I’d go with those same friends. I just want to relive that moment and hang out like we used to.”

三、Part 3: Theatres & Actors

Q1: What kinds of shows are popular in your country?

“I’d say stand-up comedy, live music, and e-sports tournaments are really popular with young people. Traditional theatre has a smaller audience.”

Q2: How easy is it to get theatre tickets?

“It depends. Buying tickets on your phone is super easy. But if it’s a hot show, it can sell out in seconds.”

Q3: Do theatres need to attract more young people?

“Yes, I think so. Traditional theatre can feel a bit disconnected from young people’s lives. They need to update their content to match what younger audiences enjoy.”

Q4: What attracts people to become actors?

“I think the money and fame play a big part. But also the chance to step into someone else’s shoes and bring a character to life. It’s a way to express yourself and experience different lives.”

Q5: What qualities do actors need?

“You need to be resilient and willing to put in the hard work. You also need to genuinely love performing and be able to convey emotions well. The downside is actors sacrifice a lot of their personal life and are always under a microscope.”

四、核心词汇

  • e-scooter — 电动车
  • city walks — 城市漫步
  • undergrad — 本科时期
  • bustling with tourists — 熙熙攘攘
  • squeezing in — 挤出时间
  • opened the door to — 打开了…的大门
  • insanely satisfying — 超级过瘾
  • carefree — 无忧无虑
  • relive that moment — 重温那一刻
  • sell out in seconds — 秒售罄
  • disconnected from — 与…脱节
  • step into someone’s shoes — 代入他人角色
  • resilient — 坚韧
  • under a microscope — 被放大审视
